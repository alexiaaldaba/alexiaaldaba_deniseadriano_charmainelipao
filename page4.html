 The "'D Recycling Garbage" is a website that talks about on how to protect our surroundings and how to be responsible on everything we do. This website is here to be the voice of mother nature to help her in saving our planet from people who are destroying it. While we, the developers will give you some helpful tips on how to reduce, reuse, and recycle, and the different ways on how to clean our society.
We, the developers chose this propaganda, because we all know that most of us is aware of how our nature is slowly dying but chose to ignore it. We are here to encourage everyone to be part of this project; we may be apart from each other uet we are going to work as one.
Most of us experienced how cruel is nature that it comes to the point it took the means of livelihood of someone and unfortunately people's lives. Example of this is the Santo Rosario, Malolos, Bulacan. When heavy rainfall pours down, every drainage system is tested to its limit. Clogged canals and water passageways are primarily the reason why it floods. The moon sets and the sun takes over, yet time is clearly not of the essence since water remains stagnant resulting in the attraction of leptospirosis, dengue and other serious illness that may lead to their deathbed.
-Charmaine Lipao, Alexia Aldaba and Denise Adriano
